🥒 Drill Season

PICKLEBALL

Five drills. No excuses. Touch the kitchen.

01
Cross Court Dinking
Soft hands, diagonal angles, zero ego. Rally cross-court from the kitchen line until someone gets impatient — don't be that person. Patience is the whole drill.
Kitchen Craft
02
5 and Alive
Stay in the rally for 5 consecutive dinks, then you're allowed to rip. Miss before five? Start over. Build the streak or build character — either way, you're winning.
Mental Reps
03
Deep Serves & Returns
Aim for the last 2 feet of the court. Every. Single. Time. A deep serve is basically free rent in your opponent's head — collect that bag consistently.
Baseline Power
04
Volley Control Drills
Hands up, paddle neutral, absorb the pace. Work rapid-fire volleys at the net — block, redirect, reset. Your wrist is not the boss here. Control is.
Net Dominance
05
Overhead Smashes w/ Angle
Jump, reach, and send it cross-court into the corner with intent. Smashing straight down the middle is cowardly. Pick an angle, commit, and watch them scramble. Main character energy only.
Chaos Unleashed
Pro tip: Hydrate. Stretch. Don't trash talk
until drill 3. That's just courtesy.
