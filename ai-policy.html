Our AI
Policy

Last updated: March 2025

We believe that a human touch cannot be replaced. Every training program I write, every fitness assessment I conduct, and every coaching decision I make is guided by a real human — not an algorithm.

Our Position

Artificial intelligence is a welcome powerful tool, and we don't shy away from using it where it makes sense. But when it comes to your motivation, your performance, and your results — we draw a line. Strength training and conditioning program design requires nuanced human judgment that AI cannot replicate.

This policy outlines exactly where we use AI, and where we never will.

❌ AI is never used for

Coaching & Programming

  • Strength & conditioning program design
  • Training load and periodization decisions
  • Fitness testing and performance assessment
  • Movement analysis and technique feedback
  • Injury risk screening and modification
  • Individual athlete interpretation and coaching
  • Progress tracking and program adjustments
✓ AI may be used for

Operations & Admin

  • Marketing copy and social media content
  • Website design and development
  • Scheduling and administrative tasks
  • Email drafting and communications
  • Graphic design and visual assets
  • Research summaries and background reading
  • Business operations support
🤝

Our Human-First Commitment

We formally commit that all program design, fitness testing, and athletic programming will always be created, reviewed, and delivered by qualified human coaches and professionals.

No training plan will ever be generated or modified by an AI system without full human authorship and oversight. When you work with us, you work with a person — someone who understands your goals, your body, and your journey.

This commitment is made to every client, athlete, and partner we serve.

Questions?

If you have any questions about how we use technology in our practice, or want to learn more about our coaching philosophy, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're an open book.
